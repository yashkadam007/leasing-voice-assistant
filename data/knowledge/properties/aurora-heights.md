# Aurora Heights

## Property Overview

Aurora Heights is a transit-friendly apartment community in San Francisco with a rooftop lounge, in-building fitness, and homes near Market Street. The community is a good fit for renters who want city access and shared amenity spaces.

## Amenities

Amenities at Aurora Heights include a rooftop lounge, fitness room, controlled-entry lobby, package lockers, and elevator access. Select homes include balconies or bay views.

## Pet Policy

Aurora Heights welcomes cats and dogs, up to two pets per home. Breed restrictions apply, and prospects should confirm current pet rent or pet deposit details with the leasing team.

## Parking

Aurora Heights offers garage parking for $275 per month. Parking availability can change, so the caller should confirm a space when selecting a unit.

## Deposits And Fees

Aurora Heights currently lists a $55 application fee and a $750 security deposit. Final deposit requirements can depend on the approved application.

## Lease Terms

Aurora Heights offers 9, 12, and 15 month lease terms.
