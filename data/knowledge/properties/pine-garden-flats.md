# Pine Garden Flats

## Property Overview

Pine Garden Flats is a quiet garden-style community in Oakland near Lake Merritt and local cafes. The property is suited for renters who prefer a smaller community feel and outdoor green space.

## Amenities

Amenities at Pine Garden Flats include landscaped courtyards, shared laundry, bike storage, and easy access to neighborhood dining. Select homes include patios or extra storage.

## Pet Policy

Pine Garden Flats allows one cat or dog per home with monthly pet rent. Prospects should confirm current pet rent and any restrictions before applying.

## Parking

Pine Garden Flats includes one surface parking space with each lease.

## Deposits And Fees

Pine Garden Flats currently lists a $45 application fee and a $500 security deposit. Final deposit requirements can depend on the approved application.

## Lease Terms

Pine Garden Flats offers 12 month lease terms.
