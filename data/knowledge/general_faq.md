# General Leasing FAQ

## Application Process

Prospects can apply online after choosing a home or community. The application asks for contact information, rental history, income information, and consent for screening. A leasing specialist follows up if anything is missing before approval.

## Application Fees

Each adult applicant pays a non-refundable application fee. Aurora Heights currently lists a $55 application fee, and Pine Garden Flats currently lists a $45 application fee.

## Deposits

Security deposits vary by community and approved application. Aurora Heights currently lists a $750 security deposit. Pine Garden Flats currently lists a $500 security deposit.

## Pet Policy

Cats and dogs are welcome at participating communities. Pet limits, monthly pet rent, deposits, and breed restrictions can vary by property, so callers should confirm the exact property before relying on pet details.

## Parking Policy

Parking options vary by property. Some communities include a parking space, while others offer garage parking for a monthly charge. The leasing assistant should answer with the property-specific parking policy when the caller names a community.

## Lease Terms

Lease terms vary by property and current availability. Aurora Heights offers 9, 12, and 15 month lease terms. Pine Garden Flats offers 12 month lease terms.
