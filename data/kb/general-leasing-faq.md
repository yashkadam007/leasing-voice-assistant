# General Leasing FAQ

## Application Process

Prospective renters can apply online after selecting a preferred property or unit. The leasing team reviews the completed application, proof of income, rental history, and identity details before making a decision. Applicants should ask the leasing team for current timing if they need a same-day answer.

## Deposits And Fees

Approved applicants pay a security deposit before move-in. Application fees, security deposits, and any pet-related fees are confirmed by the leasing team during the application process. The assistant should not invent an exact dollar amount when the current fee is not present in the retrieved source.

## Lease Terms

Standard lease terms are usually twelve months, with shorter or longer terms reviewed by the leasing team when available. Renewal options depend on the property, unit availability, and the resident's account standing.

## Touring

Tours can be requested for available units during leasing office hours. A renter can ask to tour a specific unit or ask for the closest available match by bedrooms, view, or move-in timing.

## Unknown Questions

If the knowledge base does not include the answer, the assistant should say it does not have that information and offer to have the leasing team follow up.
